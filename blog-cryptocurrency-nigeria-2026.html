üìã How to Buy Bitcoin in Nigeria

Step-by-Step Guide (Binance P2P):

  1. Create account: Download Binance app, sign up with email
  2. Complete KYC: Upload NIN, passport, or driver's license
  3. Go to P2P: Tap "P2P Trading" in the app
  4. Select "Buy": Choose USDT or BTC
  5. Find seller: Look for verified sellers with good ratings
  6. Place order: Enter amount in Naira
  7. Transfer funds: Send Naira to seller's bank account
  8. Confirm: Once seller releases, crypto appears in your wallet
üí° P2P Safety Tips:
  • Only trade with verified sellers (diamond badge)
  • Check completion rate (95%+ is good)
  • Never release crypto before confirming payment
  • Keep all transactions within the platform

üí∞ Which Crypto to Buy?

  • Bitcoin (BTC): The "digital gold" - best for long-term holding
  • USDT/USDC: Stablecoins pegged to USD - protect against Naira devaluation
  • Ethereum (ETH): Second largest - good for diversification
  • BNB: Binance coin - reduced fees on Binance
