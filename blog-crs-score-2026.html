How to Improve Your CRS Score in 2026

Updated January 2026 ‚Ä¢ 10 min read

Your CRS score (Comprehensive Ranking System) determines when you'll receive an Invitation to Apply for Canadian permanent residence. Higher score = earlier invitation.

Here are proven strategies to boost your score.

Advertisement

Fastest Ways to Increase CRS

1. Improve Your Language Scores (Up to +40 points)

Language scores have the highest impact. Target CLB 9+ (IELTS 8.0) in each band.

  • Practice with official IELTS materials
  • Take the test multiple times if needed
  • Consider online IELTS prep courses

2. Get a Provincial Nomination (+600 points)

A Provincial Nominee Program (PNP) nomination guarantees an ITA. Research streams that match your profile:

  • Ontario Human Capital Priorities
  • BC Tech Pilot
  • Saskatchewan Express Entry
  • Alberta Advantage Immigration
Advertisement

3. Learn French (+50 points)

Bilingual candidates get significant bonus points. Even intermediate French (CLB 5+) helps.

4. Get Canadian Work Experience

One year of Canadian experience through a work permit adds 40-80 points plus makes you eligible for Canadian Experience Class.

5. Complete Canadian Education

A 1-2 year Canadian diploma or degree adds 15-30 points.
