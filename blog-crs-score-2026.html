‚ö° Fastest CRS Boosters (Ranked)

#1: Provincial Nomination (PNP) +600 POINTS

The BIGGEST boost. Getting nominated by a Canadian province adds 600 points instantly.

  • Saskatchewan, Manitoba, Nova Scotia often have lower requirements
  • Some provinces don't require job offer
  • Processing: 3-6 months
#2: Improve IELTS to CLB 9+ +40 POINTS

Getting CLB 9 (IELTS 8.0 in each band) vs CLB 7 adds up to 40 points.

  • L: 8.0, R: 7.0, W: 7.0, S: 7.0 = CLB 9
  • Each band increase matters significantly
  • Worth investing in IELTS prep
#3: Learn French (TEF/TCF) +30-50 POINTS

French proficiency with strong English adds 30-50 bonus points.

üìä CRS Points Breakdown

Core Factors (Max 460 with spouse, 500 single)

  • Age: Max 110 points (peaks at 20-29)
  • Education: Max 150 points (PhD = most)
  • Language: Max 136 points (CLB 10 = most)
  • Work experience: Max 80 points (5+ years)

Additional Factors (Max 600)

  • Canadian education: +15-30 points
  • Sibling in Canada: +15 points
  • French + English: +25-50 points
  • Job offer: +50-200 points
  • PNP nomination: +600 points
