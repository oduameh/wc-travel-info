üë∑ Employee Work Visa (Salari√©)

For those with job offers not qualifying for Talent Passport, the standard employee visa is available.

Process

  1. French employer obtains work authorization (autorisation de travail)
  2. DIRECCTE approves after labor market test
  3. Apply for long-stay visa at French consulate
  4. Receive visa and travel to France
  5. Register with OFII within 3 months

Requirements

  • Valid job offer from French employer
  • Employer must prove unable to find local candidate
  • Salary meeting minimum wage (SMIC) requirements
  • Qualifications matching the position
