Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2-3 business days. For free resource requests, please allow up to 48 hours for delivery.

Please note that we receive many emails and prioritize those with clear, specific questions. Emails requesting immigration advice will receive a standard response directing you to official resources.

Report Fraud

If someone is claiming to represent this website and offering immigration services, processing applications, or guaranteeing visas, they are committing fraud. Please report:

  • To us at info@canadamexicovisa.info
  • To the College of Immigration and Citizenship Consultants
  • To local law enforcement
