üìã Express Entry Step-by-Step

Step 1: Take the IELTS (2-3 months)

IELTS General Training is required. Target scores for competitive CRS:

  • Listening: 8.0+ (gives maximum points)
  • Reading: 7.0+
  • Writing: 7.0+
  • Speaking: 7.0+

üìñ Read: IELTS Preparation Guide ‚Üí

Step 2: Get Your Education Assessed (1-2 months)

World Education Services (WES) is the most common choice:

  1. Request transcripts from your Nigerian university
  2. Send to WES Canada (not WES USA!)
  3. Pay $300 for evaluation
  4. Receive ECA report in 4-8 weeks

Step 3: Create Express Entry Profile

Submit your profile on the IRCC website with:

  • IELTS scores
  • WES ECA report
  • Work experience details
  • Personal information

Step 4: Wait for ITA (Invitation to Apply)

IRCC conducts draws every 2 weeks. If your CRS score meets the cutoff, you'll receive an ITA.

Step 5: Submit PR Application

You have 60 days to submit your complete application with all documents.

üìä How to Calculate Your CRS Score

The Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS) scores you out of 1,200 points:

Core/Human Capital Factors (max 500)
  • Age: Up to 110 points (max at 20-29)
  • Education: Up to 150 points (PhD gets max)
  • Language: Up to 136 points (CLB 10 = max)
  • Canadian Experience: Up to 80 points
Skill Transferability (max 100)

Bonus points for combinations of education + language, work experience + language, etc.

Additional Points (max 600) Key!
  • Provincial Nomination: 600 points (game changer!)
  • Valid job offer: 50-200 points
  • Canadian education: 15-30 points
  • French language: 25-50 points
  • Sibling in Canada: 15 points
üí° Strategy for Lower CRS Scores: Apply for Provincial Nominee Programs (PNP). A nomination adds 600 points, guaranteeing an ITA. Popular PNPs for Nigerians: Ontario OINP, Alberta AINP, British Columbia BC PNP.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a job offer to apply? +

No! The Federal Skilled Worker program doesn't require a job offer. You can apply based on your education, work experience, language skills, and age alone. A job offer adds 50-200 CRS points but is optional.

How long is the entire process? +

Typically 8-14 months from starting IELTS prep to landing in Canada. Breakdown: IELTS (2-3 months), WES (1-2 months), profile creation and ITA wait (2-6 months), PR processing (2-6 months).

Can my spouse and children come with me? +

Yes! Your spouse and dependent children under 22 can be included in your PR application. Your spouse's credentials can even boost your CRS score.

What happens if my CRS score is too low? +

Options: 1) Apply for PNP nomination (+600 points), 2) Improve IELTS score, 3) Gain more work experience, 4) Get a Canadian education, 5) Learn French, 6) Wait for category-based draws with lower cutoffs.
