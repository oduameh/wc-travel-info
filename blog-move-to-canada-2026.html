How much does it cost to move to Canada from Nigeria in 2026? +

What is the minimum CRS score for Canada PR in 2026? +

General Express Entry draws have cutoffs around 470-510. However, category-based draws (healthcare, STEM, trades, French) have lower cutoffs of 400-450. Provincial Nominee Programs (PNP) add 600 points, making any score above 300 viable.

Can I move to Canada without a job offer? +

Yes! Express Entry's Federal Skilled Worker Program does NOT require a job offer. You immigrate based on your education, work experience, language skills, and age. A job offer adds 50-200 points but is not mandatory.

How long does Canada immigration take from Nigeria? +

The complete process takes 8-14 months: IELTS prep (2-3 months), WES evaluation (2-3 months), Express Entry profile (1 week), waiting for ITA (1-6 months), PR application processing (4-6 months).

What IELTS score do I need for Canada Express Entry? +

Minimum is CLB 7 (6.0 in all bands). However, aim for CLB 9+ (L:8.0, R:7.0, W:7.0, S:7.0) to maximize CRS points and be competitive. Higher IELTS = faster invitation.

Is Express Entry free to apply? +

Creating an Express Entry profile is FREE. You only pay when you receive an ITA and submit your PR application. Avoid agents who charge to "submit" your Express Entry‚Äîyou can do it yourself on the IRCC website.
