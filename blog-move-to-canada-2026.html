üìù Step-by-Step Process

1

Take IELTS General Training

Book at British Council Nigeria. Target CLB 9 (L:8.0, R:7.0, W:7.0, S:7.0) for maximum points. Prepare for 8-12 weeks.

2

Get Educational Credential Assessment (ECA)

Use WES Canada (most popular). Send transcripts from your university. Takes 4-8 weeks for processing.

3

Create Express Entry Profile

Free submission at ircc.gc.ca. Enter your details to get your CRS score. You'll be placed in the candidate pool.

4

Receive Invitation to Apply (ITA)

IRCC conducts draws every 2 weeks. If your score meets the cutoff, you'll get an ITA. Valid for 60 days.

5

Submit PR Application

Upload all documents: medical, police certificate, proof of funds, work reference letters. Pay the $1,365 fee.

6

Get COPR & Land in Canada!

Confirmation of Permanent Residence arrives in 2-6 months. Book your flight and start your new life! üéâ

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply without a job offer? +

Yes! Express Entry (Federal Skilled Worker) doesn't require a job offer. You need 67+ points on the FSW grid based on age, education, language, and experience. A job offer adds 50-200 CRS points but isn't mandatory.

How long does the entire process take? +

Timeline: IELTS prep (2-3 months) + WES evaluation (1-2 months) + Pool wait (2-6 months) + PR processing (2-6 months) = 8-14 months total. Some lucky candidates complete it in 6 months!

Can my family come with me? +

Yes! Your spouse and children under 22 can be included in your PR application. You'll need proof of funds for the entire family (increases with family size). Additional fees apply per person.

What if I don't have proof of funds? +

You need to show the funds for at least 6 months before applying. Options: save gradually, get family support, or consider the Provincial Nominee Program (some provinces have lower/no POF requirements for job offer holders).
