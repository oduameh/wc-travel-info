About VisaPathway

Expert immigration and finance guides for Nigerians

Our Mission

VisaPathway provides free, accurate, and up-to-date information to help Nigerians make informed decisions about immigration, relocation, and building wealth.

We believe that access to quality information shouldn't depend on your ability to pay expensive consultants. Our guides break down complex visa processes into clear, actionable steps with costs in Naira.

What We Provide

  • Immigration Guides: Step-by-step processes for Canada, Germany, UK, Ireland, and more
  • Cost Breakdowns: Real costs in Naira, including hidden fees
  • Finance Guides: Cryptocurrency, forex, dollar accounts, and remote work opportunities
  • Document Checklists: What you need and where to get it
⚠️ Important Disclaimer

VisaPathway is an independent educational resource. We are NOT:

  • A government agency or embassy
  • A licensed immigration consultant
  • A law firm
  • Able to process visa applications

Our content is for informational purposes only. Immigration laws change frequently — always verify with official government sources before making decisions.

Our Values

Accuracy

We research thoroughly and update our guides regularly. If we're not sure about something, we say so.

Transparency

We're honest about costs, timelines, and requirements. No false promises of "guaranteed visas."

Accessibility

Our guides are free. We use ads to support the site, but the information remains accessible to everyone.
