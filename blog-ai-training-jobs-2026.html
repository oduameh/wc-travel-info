Do I need a degree for AI training jobs? +

No! Most platforms care about skills, not degrees. However, degrees/expertise in STEM, writing, or law can qualify you for higher-paying specialized tasks.

How stable is this income? +

Work availability varies. Best approach: sign up for 2-3 platforms to ensure consistent tasks. Build good ratings for priority access to higher-paying projects.

Technically, all income is taxable. Self-employed individuals should register and pay taxes. However, enforcement on small remote income is limited. Keep records in case.
