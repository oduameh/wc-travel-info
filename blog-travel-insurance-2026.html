‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a refund if my visa is rejected? +

Most standard policies don't cover visa rejection. However, some providers offer refunds if you cancel before the trip start date. Heymondo and AXA offer partial refunds. For full protection, look for "Cancel For Any Reason" (CFAR) coverage.

Is travel insurance mandatory for all visas? +

It's mandatory for Schengen visa. For UK, US, and Canada visitor visas, it's not required but strongly recommended. For Canada Super Visa, it's mandatory with specific requirements.

Can I use Nigerian insurance companies? +

Some Nigerian insurers partner with international providers (like AXA via local agents). However, for visa purposes, it's safer to use internationally recognized companies. Embassy staff may not recognize local-only insurers.

Do I need insurance if I have a credit card with travel benefits? +

Credit card travel insurance usually doesn't meet Schengen requirements (coverage too low, no certificate provided). You'll likely need separate insurance for visa applications. However, card benefits can supplement your purchased policy.
