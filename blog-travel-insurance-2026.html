Travel Insurance for Different Visa Types

Schengen Visa Insurance Requirements

For European Schengen visas, your travel insurance MUST have:

  • Minimum ‚Ç¨30,000 medical coverage
  • Repatriation/medical evacuation coverage
  • Valid for all Schengen countries
  • Cover entire duration of stay + 15 days

Canada Visitor Visa Insurance

Not mandatory but strongly recommended. Canadian healthcare is NOT free for visitors. A simple ER visit can cost $500-2,000.

USA Visitor Visa Insurance

Not required but essential. US medical costs are the highest in the world. Get at least $100,000 coverage.

UK Visa Insurance

Not mandatory for short visits. Recommended for stays over 6 months as NHS access is limited.

How to Choose the Right Policy

  1. Check visa requirements: Some countries mandate specific coverage levels
  2. Consider your activities: Adventure sports need special coverage
  3. Check medical limits: Minimum $50,000 for most countries, $100,000+ for USA
  4. Read exclusions: Pre-existing conditions often excluded
  5. Compare quotes: Use comparison sites to find best rates

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does travel insurance cost for Nigerians?

For a 2-week trip: $30-150 depending on destination and coverage. Schengen visa insurance from ‚Ç¨0.99/day. Annual multi-trip policies cost $150-400. Prices vary by age, destination, and coverage level.

Can I buy travel insurance with a Nigerian card?

Yes. Most international providers accept Nigerian Mastercard and Visa cards. Some also accept PayPal. SafetyWing and World Nomads are popular choices that work well with Nigerian payment methods.

Is travel insurance required for Canadian visa?

Not mandatory for visitor visas, but strongly recommended. For Super Visa (parents/grandparents), you MUST have minimum $100,000 Canadian medical insurance from a Canadian provider.

Does travel insurance cover COVID-19?

Most policies now cover COVID-19 medical treatment. Check if your policy covers: (1) COVID treatment abroad, (2) quarantine accommodation, (3) trip cancellation due to COVID. Not all policies include all three.
