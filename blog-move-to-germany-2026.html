‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to speak German to move to Germany? +

Not necessarily. For the Opportunity Card, English B2 gives you 1 point. For EU Blue Card, many IT and international companies use English as working language. However, learning German (even A1-A2) dramatically improves your job prospects and life quality.

Can I bring my family to Germany? +

Yes! With the Opportunity Card, your spouse can come with you AND work. With EU Blue Card, your spouse gets automatic work permit. Children can attend free German schools.

How long until I get German citizenship? +

You can apply for German citizenship after 5 years of residence (reduced to 3 years with excellent integration). You'll need German B1 level and pass a citizenship test. Germany recently started allowing dual citizenship!

Job Seeker Visa: No work allowed. Opportunity Card: Yes! 20 hours/week allowed. This is a major advantage of the new Opportunity Card‚Äîyou can earn money while searching for your main job.
