‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring my family to Germany? +

Yes! With EU Blue Card, family can join immediately. With other visas, they can apply for family reunification once you're settled and can prove income and housing.

Do I need to speak German? +

For IT and tech jobs, English is often sufficient. For Blue Card and skilled worker visas, no German requirement initially. But A1/B1 level helps for daily life and faster PR.

How long until I get permanent residency? +

EU Blue Card: 21 months with B1 German, 33 months with A1. Other work visas: typically 4-5 years. Citizenship possible after 5-8 years.
