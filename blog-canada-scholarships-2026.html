üìã IELTS Requirements

Most Canadian universities and scholarship programs require English proficiency:

  • Minimum: IELTS 6.5 overall (no band below 6.0)
  • Competitive: IELTS 7.0+ for top scholarships like Vanier
  • Alternatives: TOEFL iBT 90+, Duolingo 115+, PTE Academic 65+
