Major international sporting events have always sparked global curiosity about host nations. When South Africa hosted in 2010, millions discovered the Rainbow Nation. Qatar 2022 introduced the world to Gulf culture. Now, North America prepares to welcome the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

When and where is the 2026 World Cup? +

The 2026 FIFA World Cup will be held from June 11 to July 19, 2026 across the United States, Canada, and Mexico. This marks the first time three countries will co-host the tournament. The USA will host 60 matches including the final, while Canada and Mexico will each host 10 matches.

Which cities in Canada will host matches? +

Toronto and Vancouver are Canada's two host cities. Toronto's BMO Field and Vancouver's BC Place will host group stage matches and potentially knockout rounds.

Which cities in Mexico will host matches? +

Mexico will host matches in three cities: Mexico City (Estadio Azteca), Guadalajara (Estadio Akron), and Monterrey (Estadio BBVA). The Estadio Azteca is the only stadium to have hosted two World Cup finals (1970 and 1986).

How many teams will participate? +

The 2026 World Cup will feature 48 teams ‚Äî an increase from the traditional 32. This expansion means more countries will have the opportunity to qualify and compete on the world's biggest football stage.

Will Nigeria qualify for the 2026 World Cup? +

African qualifying for the 2026 World Cup is ongoing. Africa has been allocated 9 direct qualification spots (up from 5 previously). Nigeria's Super Eagles are among the teams competing to secure one of these spots. Follow official CAF and FIFA announcements for the latest qualifying updates.

How can I get official World Cup tickets? +

Official tickets are sold exclusively through FIFA.com. FIFA typically releases tickets in phases, including random selection draws for high-demand matches. Be cautious of unofficial sellers ‚Äî only purchase through FIFA's official platform to avoid scams.

Note: This website does not sell tickets or provide ticket access.

What visas are needed to visit Canada or Mexico? +

Visa requirements depend on your nationality and purpose of visit. Nigerian citizens generally require visas for both Canada and Mexico. Requirements, processes, and timelines vary and are determined by each country's immigration authorities.

Trip cancellation insurance protects your investment if you need to cancel for covered reasons. This is especially important for expensive international trips involving flights, accommodations, and event tickets.

üß≥

Understanding Immigration Processes

For those considering longer stays or relocation, understanding immigration pathways is important. Both Canada and Mexico have specific processes for visitors, workers, and potential immigrants.

üá®üá¶ Canada Immigration Overview

Canada offers several immigration programs including:

  • Express Entry for skilled workers
  • Provincial Nominee Programs (PNP)
  • Study permits leading to work permits
  • Visitor visas for tourism and short stays
  • Family sponsorship programs

Many people consult with a licensed immigration lawyer or immigration consultant to understand their options and improve their applications.

üá≤üáΩ Mexico Entry Information

Mexico's immigration system includes:

  • Tourist visas and visa-free entry (varies by nationality)
  • Temporary resident visas for extended stays
  • Work permits for employment
  • Student visas for educational purposes
  • Permanent residency pathways

Requirements for Nigerian citizens differ from other nationalities. Check official Mexican consulate resources.
