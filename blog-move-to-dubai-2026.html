üìã UAE Visa Options

1. Employment/Work Visa (Most Common)

  • Requirement: Job offer from UAE employer
  • Duration: 2-3 years, renewable
  • Cost: Employer-sponsored (free for you)
  • Family: Can sponsor dependents if earning 4,000+ AED

2. Golden Visa (10-Year Residence)

  • For: Investors, entrepreneurs, skilled professionals, students
  • Skilled Worker Category: Bachelor's degree + AED 30,000+ salary
  • Duration: 10 years renewable
  • Benefit: No sponsor needed, can stay outside UAE for extended periods

3. Freelance/Self-Employment Visa

  • For: Freelancers, remote workers
  • Cost: AED 7,500-15,000/year
  • Benefit: Work for multiple clients, be your own sponsor
