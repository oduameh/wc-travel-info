Dubai and the UAE offer tax-free income, modern infrastructure, and a strategic location between Europe, Asia, and Africa. Here's how to make the move.

Visa Options

Employment Visa (Most Common)

Sponsored by your employer. They handle the visa process once you have a job offer.

  • Valid for 2-3 years
  • Employer sponsors and pays for visa
  • Can bring family members

Golden Visa (10 Years)

Long-term residence for:

  • Investors: AED 2M+ investment
  • Entrepreneurs: Successful business owners
  • Specialized talents: Scientists, doctors, engineers
  • Outstanding students: Top graduates

Freelancer/Remote Work Visa

For self-employed individuals. Work for clients worldwide while living in Dubai.

Benefits of Living in Dubai

  • No income tax: Keep 100% of your salary
  • High salaries: Competitive pay, especially in tech and finance
  • Safety: Very low crime rate
  • Modern infrastructure: World-class amenities
  • Diverse community: 90% of residents are expats
