Payoneer

  3. Deposit minimum balance ($100-500)

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Which has the best exchange rate? +

Grey.co typically offers rates closest to parallel market. Payoneer uses CBN rates + 2%. Banks use CBN rate. For best rates: Grey > Wise > Payoneer > Banks.

Can I use domiciliary for visa proof of funds? +

Yes! Domiciliary accounts are the standard for visa proof of funds. Virtual accounts (Grey, Payoneer) are sometimes not accepted by embassies.
