üìö How to Start Forex Trading

Step 1: Learn First (2-3 months minimum)

  • Watch free courses (BabyPips.com is the best)
  • Understand currency pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD)
  • Learn technical analysis (charts, indicators)
  • Understand risk management (stop loss, position sizing)

Step 2: Practice on Demo Account (1-3 months)

  • Open free demo account with any broker
  • Trade with fake money until consistently profitable
  • Track your performance in a journal

Step 3: Start Small with Real Money

  • Deposit only what you can afford to lose
  • Start with micro lots (0.01)
  • Risk max 1-2% per trade
  • Scale up slowly as you gain experience
üí° Golden Rule: If you can't make profit on demo for 3+ months consistently, you're not ready for real money. Be patient!
