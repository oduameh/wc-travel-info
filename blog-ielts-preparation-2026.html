üìÖ 8-Week Study Plan

Week 1-2: Understand the Test

Goal: Know exactly what you're facing

  • Take a diagnostic test
  • Learn test format for all 4 sections
  • Identify your weakest areas
Week 3-4: Focus on Weak Areas
  • Writing: Learn essay structures, practice daily
  • Speaking: Record yourself, practice with partner
  • Listening: Do 1 full test daily
Week 5-6: Full Practice Tests
  • Take 2-3 full tests per week
  • Simulate real conditions
  • Review ALL mistakes
Week 7-8: Final Polish
  • Perfect your Writing templates
  • Practice 50+ Speaking topics
  • Rest before test day
