üìÖ 8-Week IELTS Study Plan

Week 1-2: Understand the Test

Goal: Know exactly what you're facing

  • Take a diagnostic test (Cambridge IELTS book)
  • Learn test format for all 4 sections
  • Identify your weakest areas
  • Watch IELTS overview videos on YouTube
  • Study 2-3 hours daily
Week 3-4: Focus on Weak Areas

Goal: Intensive practice on problem areas

  • Writing: Learn essay structures, practice Task 1 & 2 daily
  • Speaking: Record yourself, practice with partner
  • Listening: Do 1 full test daily, focus on spelling
  • Reading: Time yourself strictly (20 mins/passage)
  • Study 3-4 hours daily
Week 5-6: Full Practice Tests

Goal: Build test stamina and timing

  • Take 2-3 full practice tests per week
  • Simulate real test conditions (no breaks, strict timing)
  • Review ALL mistakes‚Äîunderstand why you got them wrong
  • Focus on time management
  • Study 3-4 hours daily
Week 7-8: Final Polish

Goal: Peak performance on test day

  • Take 1 full test every 2 days
  • Perfect your Writing Task 2 templates
  • Practice Speaking topics (prepare 50+ topics)
  • Review common mistakes
  • Rest well before test day

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Academic or General Training for Canada? +

General Training for immigration (Express Entry, PNP). Academic only if you're also applying to study. General Training Reading is easier, so most score higher on it.

How many times can I take IELTS? +

Unlimited! You can take IELTS as many times as you want. Many people take it 2-3 times to improve scores. Results are valid for 2 years from test date.

Can I retake just one section? +

Yes! IELTS One Skill Retake is now available. If you scored well in 3 sections but need to improve one, you can retake just that section. Saves time and money.

How long are IELTS results valid? +

IELTS results are valid for 2 years from the test date. For Canada Express Entry, your results must be valid when you submit your profile AND when you submit your PR application after ITA.

What if I disagree with my score? +

Request Enquiry on Results (EOR) within 6 weeks. Costs ‚Ç¶35,000 per skill. If your score increases, you get a refund. Most successful for Writing and Speaking.
