How to Move to Ireland in 2026

Updated January 2026 • 10 min read

Ireland is a top destination for tech workers and skilled professionals. English-speaking, EU member, and home to major tech companies like Google, Meta, and Apple.

Advertisement

Main Work Visa Options

Critical Skills Employment Permit

Best option for high-skilled workers. Eligible occupations include IT, engineering, healthcare, and finance.

  • Minimum salary: €38,000 (€64,000 for non-critical skills list)
  • Duration: 2 years, leads to Stamp 4 (unrestricted work)
  • Spouse can work immediately

General Employment Permit

For occupations not on the Critical Skills list. Minimum salary €34,000. More restrictions but still a valid pathway.

Path to Permanent Residence

  • After 2 years on Critical Skills → Stamp 4 (no work restrictions)
  • After 5 years residence → Long-term residency
  • After 5 years → Eligible for citizenship
